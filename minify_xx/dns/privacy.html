Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use Explyra DNS.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide better services to our users:

  • Account Information: When you sign up, we collect your email address and profile information via Firebase Authentication.
  • DNS Metadata: We store the DNS records and subdomains you create to manage your infrastructure.
  • Technical Logs: We may log your IP address, browser type, and usage patterns for security, debugging, and anti-spam purposes.

2. How We Use Information

We use the collected data for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain our Service.
  • To notify you about changes to our Service.
  • To provide customer support.
  • To monitor the usage of our Service and detect/prevent technical issues or abuse.

3. Data Storage and Security

We use **Firebase** (a Google service) for data storage and authentication. Your data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

4. Third-Party Services

Our Service integrates with several third-party providers:

  • Cloudflare: For DNS management and security.
  • Vercel: For project deployments.
  • EmailJS: For sending system notifications.

These third parties have their own privacy policies governing how they handle your data.

5. Cookies

We use local storage and cookies to maintain your session and theme preferences. You can control cookie usage through your browser settings.

6. Your Data Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete the information we have on you. You can manage most of this directly through your dashboard or by contacting us.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact privacy@explyra.me
