Mitanshu Bhasin

Delhi-based developer, entrepreneur, and now — author. Mitanshu is the founder of Explyra, a platform that helps professionals showcase their work experience. He writes the same way he codes: late at night, with too much coffee, and a stubborn insistence on honesty. The Code of Heartbreak is his debut — a book he wrote while building his startup and learning, simultaneously, what it feels like to lose something you love and keep going anyway.
