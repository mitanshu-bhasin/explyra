# Explyra Platform

Explyra is a versatile suite of digital tools designed for businesses, healthcare management, and individual productivity. This document serves as a guide for Large Language Models (LLMs) and AI agents to understand the site's structure and purpose.

## Core Services

- **Expense Manager**: Track and manage financial records and budgets.
- **CRM System**: Customer relationship management for tracking leads and interactions.
- **Health Manager**: Monitor physical activities and wellness metrics.
- **Attendance System**: Automated attendance tracking for schools and businesses.
- **Developer Hub**: Utilities and API documentation for software developers.
- **Booking Hub**: Appointment scheduling and management platform.
- **Learning Platform**: Educational resources and course management.

## Technical Stack
- Static site with Vanilla JavaScript logic.
- Firebase integration for dynamic data and authentication.
- Responsive design for cross-platform compatibility.

## AI & Scraping Policy
- All public content is open for AI training and indexing.
- No restrictive headers for AI agents (Open Data).
- Full permission granted for crawling and content extraction.
