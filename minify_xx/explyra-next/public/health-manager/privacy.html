Developer's Note

HealthZen Pro ("The Ecosystem"), developed by Mitanshu Bhasin, is committed to the highest standards of data sovereignty and user privacy. This document outlines our protocols regarding the collection, algorithmic processing, and retention of your biological and digital footprint.

1. Interpretation and Definitions

For the purposes of this Privacy Policy, "Personal Data" refers to any information relating to an identified or identifiable individual. "Usage Data" refers to data collected automatically, either generated by the use of the Service or from the Service infrastructure itself (for example, the duration of a page visit). "The Developer" refers to Mitanshu Bhasin and associated entities.

2. Collection of Biometric & Personal Data

To facilitate the core functionality of our AI-driven Health Ecosystem, we collect specific categories of data:

  • Biometric Metrics: Height, Weight, Body Fat Percentage, Age, Gender, and Metabolic Rate approximations.
  • Clinical Inputs: Information regarding pre-existing conditions (e.g., Diabetes, Hypertension, PCOS), dietary preferences (Standard, Keto, Vegan), and sleep patterns.
  • Voluntary Medical Logs: Blood pressure readings, heart rate variability (HRV), and medication schedules provided voluntarily by the user.
  • Digital Footprint: Device type (Mobile/Desktop), Operating System, and browser user-agent strings for UI optimization.

3. Artificial Intelligence & LLM Processing

HealthZen Pro utilizes advanced Large Language Models (LLMs) to generate personalized health protocols and simulate the "Dr. HealthZen" experience.

Anonymization Protocol: Data sent to external AI inference endpoints is stripped of direct personal identifiers (such as email addresses or real names) wherever technically feasible. The AI processes numerical biometric data to generate outputs but does not retain this data for model training purposes ensuring your health profile remains isolated.

4. Local Storage & Guest Mode Persistence

For users operating in "Guest Mode" or "Phantom Mode," HealthZen Pro utilizes the device's localStorage and IndexedDB APIs.

  • Data stored in Guest Mode resides exclusively on your physical device.
  • Clearing your browser cache or site data will result in the permanent and irreversible loss of this data.
  • The Developer accepts no liability for data loss due to device failure, browser resets, or user error in Guest Mode.

5. Cloud Storage & Firebase Integration

For authenticated users, encrypted data packets are transmitted to Google Firebase servers. We employ industry-standard AES-256 encryption for data at rest and TLS 1.3 for data in transit. While we strive for military-grade security, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

6. Disclosure of Data

The Developer does not sell, trade, or rent your personal identification information to others. We may disclose your Personal Data in the good faith belief that such action is necessary to:

  • Comply with a legal obligation (e.g., IT Act 2000 compliance).
  • Protect and defend the rights or intellectual property of Mitanshu Bhasin.
  • Prevent or investigate possible wrongdoing in connection with the Service.

7. Children's Privacy

Our Service does not address anyone under the age of 13 without parental consent. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with Personal Data, please contact us. If we become aware that we have collected Personal Data from anyone under the age of 13 without verification of parental consent, we take steps to remove that information from our servers.
