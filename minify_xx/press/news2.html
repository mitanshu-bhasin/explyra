Explyra Announces Global Expansion and Edge Infrastructure Deployment

MB
Mitanshu Bhasin Published February 28, 2026

London, UK — Explyra, the emerging leader in unified SaaS ecosystems, today announced a massive expansion of its global digital footprint. The initiative includes the deployment of 24 new edge data centers across Europe, North America, and Southeast Asia.

This infrastructure expansion is designed to support the increasing demand for Explyra's Intelligent Workflows services, which require ultra-low latency to provide real-timeSpend intelligence and CRM forecasting. By bringing processing power closer to the user, Explyra is slashing data transit times by up to 60%.

Decentralized AI Architecture

The new edge network isn't just about speed; it's about data sovereignty and privacy. Each node in the Explyra network is equipped with localized AI processing capabilities, allowing sensitive enterprise data to be analyzed without ever leaving the regional jurisdiction.

"Global scale requires local focus," says Mitanshu Bhasin, Founder of Explyra. "Our customers shouldn't have to choose between advanced AI capabilities and data privacy. With our new edge infrastructure, they get both."

Paving the Way for Enterprise ERP

This expansion also lays the foundation for the upcoming global rollout of the Explyra ERP Suite. The edge network will handle the massive data streams generated by real-time inventory tracking, geofenced attendance systems, and decentralized mailbox ecosystems.

Explyra has partnered with leading network providers to ensure 99.99% uptime and redundant routing across its global mesh. The deployment is expected to be fully operational by the end of Q3 2026.
