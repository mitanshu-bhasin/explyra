Service FAQ

Find answers to common questions about our technical solutions.

What is the typical project timeline?

Project timelines vary depending on complexity. Small audits take 1-2 weeks, while full SaaS development projects typically span 3-6 months. We provide detailed milestones for every project.

Do you offer post-deployment support?

Yes, all our services include a dedicated maintenance period. We also offer ongoing support SLAs for infrastructure management and feature updates.

How do you handle data security?

Security is baked into our DNA. We follow industry standards like SOC2 and GDPR. All our code undergoes rigorous security audits before it ever hits production.

Can you integrate with existing teams?

Absolutely. We often work as an extension of internal engineering teams, providing specialized expertise in cloud, security, or AI without disrupting your workflow.
