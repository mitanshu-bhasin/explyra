GDPR Compliance

Your Data, Your Rights, Our Commitment.

Introduction

At Explyra, we take data privacy and security very seriously. This document outlines how we comply with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) to ensure the protection of our European Union (EU) users' data and provide transparency into our data practices.

Data Protection Principles

We adhere to the core principles of GDPR in all our data processing activities:

  • Lawfulness, Fairness, and Transparency: We process data lawfully and are transparent about our methods.
  • Purpose Limitation: Data is only collected for specified, explicit, and legitimate purposes.
  • Data Minimization: We only collect the data necessary for the intended purpose.
  • Accuracy: We keep data accurate and up to date.
  • Storage Limitation: We only keep data as long as necessary.
  • Integrity and Confidentiality: We use robust security measures to protect data.

User Rights under GDPR

Under GDPR, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right to Access: You can request a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • Right to Rectification: You can ask us to correct inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Right to Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten"): You can request that we delete your data under certain conditions.
  • Right to Restrict Processing: You can request that we limit how we use your data.
  • Right to Data Portability: You can request that we transfer your data to another organization.
  • Right to Object: You can object to the processing of your data for specific purposes, like direct marketing.

How to Exercise Your Rights

If you wish to exercise any of your GDPR rights, please contact our Data Protection Officer at explyra@gmail.com. We will respond to your request within 30 days.

Data Breach Notification

In the unlikely event of a data breach that poses a high risk to your rights and freedoms, we will notify the appropriate supervisory authority within 72 hours and inform you without undue delay.
