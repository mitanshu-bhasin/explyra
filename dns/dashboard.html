    How to Buy a Domain

    1

    Choose a Registrar

    Select a trusted domain registrar from our list below or use your favorite platform.

    2

    Search Domain

    Use the registrar's search tool to find your preferred name and extension (.com, .io, etc).

    3

    Complete Purchase

    Add to cart, review pricing (watch for renewal rates), and complete the checkout process.

    4

    Privacy Protection

    Ensure WHOIS privacy protection is enabled to hide your personal contact information.

    5

    Connect to Explyra

    Once purchased, use our DNS Manager to point your domain here using A or CNAME records.
