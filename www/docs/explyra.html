The Ultimate SaaS Suite

Welcome to Explyra, the unified ecosystem designed to streamline your business operations. Explyra is not just a tool; it's a comprehensive platform that combines expense tracking, health management, professional invoicing, and developer utilities into one seamless experience.

Universal Account: Your Explyra credentials work across all products in the suite. Enable only the modules your team needs.

Global Settings

Configure your organization's core identity from the Centralized Dashboard. These settings cascade down to all active Explyra modules.

Organization Profile

Set your official company name, upload high-resolution logos for branding, and define your primary business address.

Localization

Define your default currency (USD, EUR, INR, etc.) and timezone. This ensures all reports and timestamps are consistent across your global team.

Team Roles & Permissions

Explyra uses a granular RBAC (Role-Based Access Control) system to ensure data security and operational efficiency.

Super Admin

Full access to all settings, billing, and user management. Can enable/disable entire modules.

Manager

Can view team reports, approve requests, and manage department-specific settings.

Employee

Standard access to use assigned tools (e.g., logging expenses or tracking health).

SSO & Enhanced Security

Protect your enterprise data with our multi-layered security protocols.

Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)

Require all users to verify their identity via SMS or Authenticator apps (Google, Authy) upon login.

Single Sign-On (SSO)

Integrate Explyra with your existing identity provider using SAML 2.0 or OpenID Connect. Support for Okta, Azure AD, and Google Workspace is built-in.

Security Policy: We recommend enforcing 2FA for all accounts with "Manager" or "Admin" privileges to prevent unauthorized access.

Audit Logs

Maintain complete transparency with comprehensive activity tracking. Every significant action within the ecosystem is recorded.

  • User Management: Track who was invited, deactivated, or had their role changed.
  • Security Events: Log failed login attempts, 2FA bypasses, and API key generation.
  • Configuration Changes: See exactly who changed organizational settings and when.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use Explyra modules independently?

Yes. While they integrate perfectly, you can choose to enable only the Expense Manager or only the Invoicing software based on your current needs.

Is my data backed up?

We perform encrypted backups every 4 hours across multiple geographic regions to ensure zero data loss.
