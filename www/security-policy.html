Security Policy

Responsible Disclosure

We take the security of our users' data seriously. If you believe you have found a security vulnerability in Explyra, we encourage you to let us know right away.

We ask that you give us a reasonable amount of time to respond to your report before making any information public and that you make every effort to avoid privacy violations, destruction of data, and interruption or degradation of our service during your research.

Reporting a Vulnerability

Please report security vulnerabilities by emailing us at info@explyra.me.

Include as much information as possible in your report, including:

  • Type of issue (CSRF, XSS, etc.)
  • The location of the vulnerability
  • A detailed description of the vulnerability
  • Steps to reproduce or a Proof of Concept

Scope

This policy applies to all Explyra web applications and services hosted on the explyra.me domain.
