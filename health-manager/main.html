Data Usage Policy

1. AI Processing

Your biometrics (Weight, Age, Body Fat) are sent anonymously to our LLM (Large Language Model) to generate personalized plans. No identifiable data is retained by the AI provider.

2. Cloud Storage

Your logs and settings are stored securely on Google Firebase servers. Only you can access this data via your authenticated login.

3. Trial Limitations

As this is a testing version, data persistence is not guaranteed indefinitely. Rate limits apply to AI usage to manage server costs.
