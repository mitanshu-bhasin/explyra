Explyra DNS & Mini Hosting

Explyra DNS is a high-performance hosting platform designed for developers who need instant subdomain creation, reliable DNS management, and seamless static site deployments. Built on a global Anycast network, it ensures your projects are always fast and reachable.

Instant Infrastructure

Claim your unique subdomain under mitanshu.tech or explyra.me and point it to any destination in seconds.

Subdomain Management

Users can create and manage unlimited subdomains. Each subdomain comes with dedicated provisioning and automatic SSL certificate orchestration.

  • Wildcard Support: Route all sub-traffic to a single gateway.
  • Instant Activation: Changes propagate globally in under 60 seconds.
  • Custom CNAMEs: Map your subdomains to Vercel, Netlify, or your own VPS.

Supported DNS Records

Explyra supports standard resource records for complete control over your traffic:

A       -> Point to an IPv4 address
AAAA   -> Point to an IPv6 address
CNAME -> Alias one name to another
MX     -> Mail exchange routing
TXT    -> Arbitrary text tags (SPF, DKIM, Verification)

Mini Hosting Workflow

Deploying your project to Explyra is a three-step process designed for speed:

  1. Connect: Link your GitHub repository or use our CLI.
  2. Configure: Select your subdomain and build settings.
  3. Deploy: Our CI/CD pipeline builds your static assets and pushes them to our global edge network.

Enterprise Security

Every project hosted on Explyra benefits from our multi-layer security stack:

  • DDoS Mitigation: Layer 7 protection at the edge.
  • HTTP/3 Support: Latest protocol for encrypted, low-latency delivery.
  • Auto-SSL: Certificates are issued via Let's Encrypt and renewed automatically.
