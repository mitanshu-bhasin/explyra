Indian AI Initiative Explyra Begins Building Modern AI Tools and SaaS Platforms

MB
Mitanshu Bhasin Published March 15, 2026

New Delhi, India — Today marks a significant milestone in the Indian technology landscape as Explyra officially announces its mission to build the most comprehensive, AI-integrated SaaS ecosystem designed for the modern era.

Explyra is not just another software company; it is a vision of unified productivity. Founded by Mitanshu Bhasin, a visionary developer and architect, Explyra aims to bridge the gap between fragmented digital tools and a seamless, intelligent workflow.

Integrated Digital Infrastructure

The core philosophy of Explyra revolves around its "Suite" model. Instead of enterprises managing ten different subscriptions for expenses, CRM, health tracking, and developer tools, Explyra provides a single source of truth. This integration is powered by agentic AI, capable of learning and adapting to specific business needs.

"The future of work isn't about having more tools; it's about having smarter, more connected ones. Explyra is the operating system for that future." — Mitanshu Bhasin

Focus on Developer Platforms

A major pillar of the Explyra initiative is its commitment to the developer community. By providing advanced APIs, infrastructure utilities, and AI-powered learning paths, Explyra is equipping the next generation of engineers with the tools needed to build digital infrastructure that scales globally.

Vision for the Future

As Explyra continues to grow, its roadmap includes expanding its edge infrastructure and deepening its AI capabilities. The goal is clear: to become the standard for digital transformation, starting from India and reaching the world.

For more information on the Explyra ecosystem and upcoming product releases, visit explyra.me.
